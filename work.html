How Volunteer Overseas Works

1

Find Your Perfect Project

Research and compare the best Projects abroad based on what matters you

Location Where's your dream destination?
Activity What do you see yourself doing?
Duration How much time do you have?
Start Dates Fixed and flexible start dates
(varies by program)

2

Apply now

Follow our easy and secure application process:

  1. 1Fill out your trip prefrence
  2. 2Tell your program why you are a good fit
  3. 3Reserve your spot with a small deposit*

* This deposit is generally a small portion of the total project fee. Your deposit secures your spot with the program and shows that you are a serious applicant. This deposit is fully refundable if you are not accepted by the program.

3

Wait to Here Great News

After you submit your application, you will hear from your program within 3- 7 business days.

Applying through Volunteer Overseas automatically qualifies you to:

  1. $Enter to win a scholarship from Volunteer Forever (up to $1,000)
  2. $Raise funds for your trip on Volunteer Forever's no-fee crowdfunding platform

Talk about great news!

4

Finalize your trip

After you've been accepted, your program will help you with the not so little details:

  • The remainder of your program fee
  • Final travel arrangements
  • Your itinerary and work schedule
  • Your medical, dietary or other personal needs
